Personalized Notebook

Description:

While taking your notes, you are able to customize your notebook paper and pen color. This is to better help you read your notes. It will also make taking notes more fun.

Parameters:

  • Color: Change the background color of the paper
  • Colordraw: Changes the color of your pen or stroke
  • Paperlinec: Changes the color of the lines on your paper
  • YSpacing: Increase and Decrease the width between the lines (ie. college rule or wide rule paper)
  • Strokeweightd: Changes the stroke weight of your pen or stroke
  • Strokeweightl: Change the stroke weight of the lines on your paper
